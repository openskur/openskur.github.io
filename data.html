Download SKUR Data

Step 1. Log into your SKUR account

Step 2. Navigate to your workspace

Step 3. Select your project and click on the View button

Step 4. Download objdiff file

In the upper right hand corner of a Diff Card, click on the gear icon. You should see several options, click on View More Info

A View More Info modal should appear with a link the objdiff file, click on it to download
